Reports use of attributes such as android.R.attr.checkable, android.R.attr.checked, android.R.attr.enabled with ColorStateList(), StateListAnimator.addState, and StateListDrawable.addState where state attributes such as android.R.attr.state_checkable, android.R.attr.state_checked, android.R.attr.state_enabled are expected.

It's easy to confuse these attributes and specify a nonexistent state.
