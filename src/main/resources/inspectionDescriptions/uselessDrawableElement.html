Reports useless or redundant elements of XML resources.

For Drawables: single-item layer-lists, insetless insets, empty shapes, and vector elements: empty paths and clip-paths, invisible paths, useless clip-paths and groups, attributes with no effect.

For layouts: suspicious combinations of margin/padding left/top/right/bottom/start/end and horizontal/vertical.

For anim, animators, state list animators: empty and single-item sets.

The inspection is provided by Mike's IDEA Extensions.
